Food & beverage products and their ingredients are dynamic systems; molecular reactions within them never cease to happen. Due to the dynamic nature of Food & beverage products, their manufacturing is very different from the manufacturing of machines or any other inert products. During the manufacture of Food & Beverages external factors (Temperature, humidity, air quality etc), internal factors (composition, ingredient interactions, age, pH etc) and processing conditions (heat treatment, pressure, filtration, blending, carbonation, filling etc) will have significant impact on the final product functionality & quality. Foirfe Product and Process solutions have experience in identifying/addressing processing effects on ingredient interactions and subsequent quality and functionality of finished products. Please contact us to discuss how we can work together to minimise reworks, product wastage and improve production “hit rate”.
