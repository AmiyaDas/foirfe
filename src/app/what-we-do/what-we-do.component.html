Processing & Product Functionality

Food & beverage products and their ingredients are dynamic systems; molecular reactions within them never cease to happen. Due to the dynamic nature of Food & beverage products, their manufacturing is very different from the manufacturing of machines or any other inert products. During the manufacture of Food & Beverages external factors (Temperature, humidity, air quality etc), internal factors (composition, ingredient interactions, age, pH etc) and processing conditions (heat treatment, pressure, filtration, blending, carbonation, filling etc) will have significant impact on the final product functionality & quality. Foirfe Product and Process solutions have experience in identifying/addressing processing effects on ingredient interactions and subsequent quality and functionality of finished products. Contact us to discuss how we can work together to minimise reworks, product wastage and improve production “hit rate”.

New product development: Science and sustainability

Every organisation implements their own unique approach to ensure a product’s success. A prominent approach for new entrants in the Food & Beverage sector is what we call the “Spearhead” approach, it can be defined loosely as all actions taken for speedy concept to commercialisation e.g., bulk production, aggressive marketing campaigns, establishing efficient supply-chain, sales and customer support teams etc. An additional prong to the “Spearhead” approach is, we believe, using science to make your business more sustainable e.g., establishing an efficient innovation & quality process, making robust production process etc. However, with the best of the intentions, it is difficult to add an additional prong due to various reasons e.g., contract manufacturing, costs involved in setting-up an efficient innovation team, unavailability of infrastructure, etc. We at Foirfe Process and Product solutions, can be that additional prong to your spearhead.
