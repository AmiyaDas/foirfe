Dr. Phanindra Kalyankar

Co-founder, Foirfe Process & Product Solutions

Dr Phanindra Kalyankar, Co-founder of Foirfe Process & Product Solutions, is an experienced Food & Beverage product and process developer. Phanindra has experience working in both academic and industrial settings. Phanindra holds a PhD in Protein Chemistry & Enzymology (Galway Mayo Institute of Technology) and a MSc in Biotechnology (UCC). Phanindra enjoys the challenge in providing simple, robust and sustainable product and process solutions whilst ensuring Food/Beverage consumer expectations are met.
